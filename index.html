Amazing Aerial Photography

Aerial photography is not only our life’s work but also a labor of love for the art of capturing a perfect image or video and doing so using the most advanced technology around. With 25 years experience in the professional photography field, our team specializes in professional aerial photography in the New England region capturing unique perspectives on a landscape, building, event, real estate.

 

Experience, Credentials and Performace

Aerial Shots combines both years of professional photographic experience with a love of technology to bring clients ultra high resolution aerial photography for a wide range of services. Our team can offer exclusive access to aerial photography that is hard to come by due to the complexities of U.S. airspace regulations. Thankfully we not only have the photography skill and tech ”know-how” but we also have a licensed and instrument reated pilot with a current UAS 107 on our team that enables us to guarantee legal and safe flights. We also have an FAA 107.29 waiver providing legal night time operation.

 

Safety, Planning, Operational Dilligence

Aerial Shots takes safety very seriously during each of our sessions. We have full liability and equipment coverage with certificates of insurance. We also follow safety measures before and during each shoot that include:

>
  • A full site survey and risk assessment.
  • Pre and post flight equipment safety checks
  • Emergency procedures review
  • Full flight operation brief before each departure
  • Confirmation of permission from property owners and FAA for flying in the area.
  • We have over 4,000 safe flight operations.
